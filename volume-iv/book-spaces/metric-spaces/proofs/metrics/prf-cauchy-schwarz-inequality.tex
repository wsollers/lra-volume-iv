\begin{theorem*}[Cauchy--Schwarz Inequality]
For every \(x,y\in\mathbb{R}^n\),
\[
  |x\cdot y|\leq |x|\,|y|.
\]
Equality holds if and only if \(x\) and \(y\) are linearly dependent.
\end{theorem*}
