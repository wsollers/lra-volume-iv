\begin{theorem*}[Equivalent Boundedness Criterion]
Let \((X,d)\) be a metric space and let \(A\subseteq X\).  Then \(A\) is
bounded if and only if there exist \(x\in X\) and \(R>0\) such that
\[
  d(x,a)\leq R
\]
for every \(a\in A\).
\end{theorem*}
