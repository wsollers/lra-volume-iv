\begin{theorem*}[Center Independence]
Let \((X,d)\) be a metric space.  If \(A\subseteq B_d(x;R)\), then for every
\(y\in X\),
\[
  A\subseteq B_d(y;R+d(x,y)+1).
\]
Thus boundedness does not depend on the chosen center.
\end{theorem*}
