\begin{theorem*}[Ball Containment by Center Distance]
Let \((X,d)\) be a metric space, let \(x,y\in X\), and let \(r,s>0\).
If \(d(x,y)+r\leq s\), then \(B_d(x;r)\subseteq B_d(y;s)\).  If
\(d(x,y)+r<s\), then \(\overline{B}_d(x;r)\subseteq B_d(y;s)\).
\end{theorem*}
