\begin{theorem*}[Ball Nesting]
Let \((X,d)\) be a metric space, let \(x\in X\), and let \(0<r\leq s\).
Then \(B_d(x;r)\subseteq B_d(x;s)\).  If \(r<s\), then also
\(\overline{B}_d(x;r)\subseteq B_d(x;s)\).
\end{theorem*}
