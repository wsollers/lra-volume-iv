\section{Operations}
\label{sec:operations}

\algebraTopicToolkit{Operations}{
Operations are functions whose outputs remain in the carrier set.  Their arity
records how many inputs are used: nullary operations select constants, unary
operations transform one element, and binary operations combine two elements.
}
