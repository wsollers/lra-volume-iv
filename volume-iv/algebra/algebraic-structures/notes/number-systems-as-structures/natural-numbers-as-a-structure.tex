\subsection{The Natural Numbers as a Structure}
\label{subsec:natural-numbers-as-a-structure}

\begin{remark*}[Exposition]
The natural numbers can be viewed as a structure whose carrier is
$\mathbb{N}$ and whose visible operations include addition and multiplication.
Depending on convention, $0$ and $1$ may be included as distinguished constants.
\end{remark*}
