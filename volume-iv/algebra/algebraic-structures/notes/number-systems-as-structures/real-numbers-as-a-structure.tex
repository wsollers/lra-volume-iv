\subsection{The Real Numbers as a Structure}
\label{subsec:real-numbers-as-a-structure}

\begin{remark*}[Exposition]
The real numbers $\mathbb{R}$ form a field under the usual addition and
multiplication.  When order is included, $\mathbb{R}$ is also an ordered field;
analytic completeness belongs to the analysis layer rather than to the bare
field signature.
\end{remark*}
