\section{Relations}
\label{sec:relations}

\algebraTopicToolkit{Relations}{
Binary relations are predicates on ordered pairs.  The structural properties
used later are reflexivity, irreflexivity, symmetry, antisymmetry, asymmetry,
transitivity, totality, equivalence, and order.
