\section{Algebraic Structures}
\label{sec:algebraic-structures}

\algebraTopicToolkit{Algebraic Structures}{
An algebraic structure is a carrier set equipped with operations and laws.
The live hierarchy here is magma, semigroup, monoid, group, abelian group,
ring, integral domain, and field.
}
