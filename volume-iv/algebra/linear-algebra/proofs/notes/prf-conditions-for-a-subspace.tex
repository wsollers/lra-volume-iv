\begin{theorem*}[Conditions for a Subspace]
Let $V$ be a vector space over $\mathbf{F}$ and let $U \subseteq V$.
Then $U$ is a subspace of $V$ if and only if:
(i) $0 \in U$;
(ii) $\forall u,v \in U\;(u+v \in U)$;
(iii) $\forall a \in \mathbf{F}\;\forall u \in U\;(au \in U)$.
\end{theorem*}
